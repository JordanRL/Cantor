# NumberFormat

This enum specifies what characters are used for the radix, the grouping delimiter, and the positive and negative signs.

## English

**Radix:** .  
**Delimeter:** ,  
**Positive:**  
**Negative:** -

- 100,000.00
- -10,00,000.00
- -1,000.00

## EnglishFinance

**Radix:** .  
**Delimeter:** ,  
**Positive:** +  
**Negative:** (#)

- +100,000.00
- (10,00,000.00)
- (1,000.00)
